OpenEBS Internship Program Details

OpenEBS is the fastest growing open source community in the Cloud Native Storage space. OpenEBS project is sponsored by MayaData Inc, a startup headquartered in the Silicon Valley, and has a large R&D team operating out of Bangalore.

We are seeking talents across the globe who are keen on working with us as an intern on open source technologies such as Kubernetes, Prometheus and OpenEBS.
